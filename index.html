Depression Meal Database

I compiled a list of meals for folks who are going through hard times emotionally (maybe even finanically!)

Dedicated to my wonderful and supportive girlfriend, who is also an keen expert on true depression meals with minimum effort. You keep me strong enough to put more effort even in my meals!

RULES:

  1. Must require minimum effort to create. Less dishware, the better!
    • Example: Any usage of a stovetop disqualifies the qualification of a depression meal.
  2. Meal should not go over the budget of $4 per meal.
  3. The more pathetic, the better!
  4. Typically should not take more than three steps to create. Five is pushing it!
