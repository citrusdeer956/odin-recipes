Cheese Sandwich (MICROWAVE ONLY)

Description

Sometimes a nice warm sandwich can be enough to fill that hole in your heart. With just the power of a microwave, you can recreate a childhood classic into a sad but filling meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 slice of cheese (Any)
  • 1 paper towel sheet

Instructions

  1. Assemble the bread and cheese into a sandwich. You can make any amount as you please. However many fit in a microwave.
  2. Place the sandwich (or sandwiches) in the microwave and set for 20 to 25 seconds or till cheese has melted.
  3. Take the meal out of the microwave and set aside to cool till it is ready to eat.
